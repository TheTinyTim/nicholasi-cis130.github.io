Our Story

Trinity's Toy Treasury is a small local business looking to achieve big goals! We think that kids should
have wonderful toys to train their skills at an early age. So all of us here wanted to help kids get those
toys and provide them at a competitive price.

About Trinity

I've lived in the Springfield my entire life. Majoring in economics and business and I knew I wanted
to open a store that would not only entertain kids but help them as they're developing their skills!
That's why I love to fill my store with not only the best toys around but as well as small little knick knacks
aimed at helping kids develop their thinking and fine motor skills! So I hope you enjoy every little
thing in my shop because they're all picked with your kids future in mind!
