About Us

Amycakes opened in August of 2009 in downtown Springfield, moving to the Walnut Street location in August of 2014 . Specializing in decorated and custom cakes and cupcakes, Amycakes is a locally-owned, family business. Everything is made-from-scratch so it tastes as moist, flavorful and delicious as it looks!
